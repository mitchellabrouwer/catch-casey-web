Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 10, 2025

Catch Casey is an educational augmented reality game for children. We take privacy seriously and are committed to protecting our users' personal information.

Information We Collect

Catch Casey does not collect any personal information. All gameplay progress, settings, and user data are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Location Data

Catch Casey uses your device's location to place AR hideouts near you. This location data is used only during gameplay, is never stored, and is never shared with third parties.

Camera Access

The camera is used solely for AR gameplay. No camera images or video are recorded, saved, or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Catch Casey is designed for children and contains no ads, in-app purchases, or marketing. We comply with all applicable children's privacy regulations, including COPPA where applicable.

International Data Transfers

Since Catch Casey operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device, no international data transfers take place.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.
